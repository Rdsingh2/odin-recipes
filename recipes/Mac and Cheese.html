Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

This is a great Mac and Cheese recipe.

You should really try it.

Ingredients
  • Macaroni
  • Feta Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Feta Cheese
Steps
  1. Boil water
  2. Cook Macaroni uncovered in a boil for 6 minutes
  3. Strain and transfer to a large bowl
  4. Mix in the rest of the ingredients
  5. Enjoy
